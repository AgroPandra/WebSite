Introduction

Mobile Platform for farmers

Agropanda is growing website and has grown to become the biggest online marketplace for agricultural products in Sri Lanka. Through our platform, we have worked with different areas of farmers and their farming communities, helping them to take part in and benefit from the fast-growing digital economy. As the leading online agriculture marketplace in Sri Lanka, Agropanda has made improving the productivity and efficiency of the agriculture industry supply chain our strategic focus. By making agricultural produce more easily discoverable and enabling the aggregation of demand, Agropanda is widening market access for farmers by increasing the potential pool of buyers and matching demand with supply. At the same time, we are enabling food to go from farm to table by connecting consumers directly to growers.

We are also committed to improving the quality and quantity of marketing. To that end, we are investing and working with like-minded partners across all parts of the food value chain on technologies ranging from smart farming to agricultural logistics to marketing and price predicting. We see great potential in agriculture. It remains an industry with one of the lowest rates of digitalization yet touches the greatest number of consumers and growers. As a technology company rooted in agriculture, building an open and inclusive digital ecosystem that benefits all lies at the core of our corporate philosophy and mission.

Literature

There are many researches ongoing in the industry in the domain of agriculture. Most of the researches related to prediction of crop are in research level, only few of them are implemented as systems but they are also not predictive they are just informative. There is no huge amount of existing system but still there are few with lack of features.

Research Problem

A substantial percentage of the inhabitants of the country depend on the agriculture. Thus, modernization of agriculture is very important and thus will lead the farmers of our country towards profit. But nowadays percentage of total GDP has been dropping. Below is some problem that we have identified for this change.

Main Objectives

Main objective of this application is developing an android application to show most profitable and suitable crops and to to help farmers achieve the revenues as for their investment

  • Crop prediction component development to predict profitable crops
  • Price prediction component development to predict future price of crops
  • Chat bot for helping farmers
  • Disease detection for crops for image processing

    • Solution

    Mobile platform that entails chat bot, crop disease detectin using image processing, crop and price prediction
